Explore Forest Park

One of the largest urban parks in the United States — right here in Saint Louis. Enjoy our excursion recommendations and find your way back to the hotel when you're ready.

Forest Park Map
📍 Forest Park, Saint Louis, Missouri  |  1,371 acres  |  Free admission
Excursion Recommendations

Four world-class destinations — all free to enter, all inside Forest Park.

🦁
Saint Louis Zoo
One of the top-ranked zoos in the nation, home to 14,000+ animals across 90 acres. Walk through exhibits featuring big cats, sea lions, penguins, primates, and much more. Stroller and wheelchair friendly.
Free Admission
🎨
Saint Louis Art Museum
Over 34,000 works spanning 5,000 years of history — from ancient Egypt to modern masters. Perched on Art Hill with one of the best views in the park. A truly stunning building inside and out.
Free Admission
📜
Missouri History Museum
Explore the story of St. Louis through immersive exhibits on the 1904 World's Fair, Lewis & Clark, Civil Rights history, and more. A fascinating deep-dive into the Gateway City's past.
Free Admission
🔬
Saint Louis Science Center
Hundreds of hands-on exhibits covering space, technology, nature, and the human body. Connected to the James S. McDonnell Planetarium — don't miss the live shows. Great for all ages.
Free Admission
Good to Know
💡
Tips from the Saint Louis Locals
  • All four attractions are completely free to enter — no tickets needed.
  • The Zoo is best visited in the morning before crowds build. Allow at least 2 hours.
  • The Art Museum sits on Art Hill — the view from the front steps is one of the best in the park.
  • The History Museum is a quieter, relaxed experience — perfect if you want something low-key.
  • The Science Center connects directly to the Planetarium — check show times when you arrive.
  • Free parking is available near each attraction throughout the park.
