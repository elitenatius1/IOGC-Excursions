Explore Forest Park

One of the largest urban parks in the United States — right here in Saint Louis. Enjoy our excursion recommendations and find your way back to the hotel when you're ready.

Forest Park Map
📍 Overview
Forest Park is located in the Central West End of St. Louis, Missouri, approximately 6 miles west of downtown St. Louis and about 10–15 miles southeast of St. Louis Lambert International Airport (STL).
Key Distances and Locations:
  • From Downtown St. Louis: Roughly a 10–15 minute drive (approx. 5.7–6 miles) west via I-64 / Hwy 40.
  • From St. Louis Airport (STL): Roughly a 15–20 minute drive (approx. 10–11 miles) southeast via I-70 or I-170 to I-64.
📍 Forest Park, Saint Louis, Missouri  |  1,371 acres  |  Free admission
Here are the Four Excursions that the Saints in Saint Louis recommending at Forest Park

Four world-class destinations — all free to enter, all inside Forest Park.

🦁
Saint Louis Zoo ← click to explore
~10–15 minute drive from downtown
One of the top-ranked zoos in the nation, home to 14,000+ animals across 90 acres. Walk through exhibits featuring big cats, sea lions, penguins, primates, and much more. Stroller and wheelchair friendly.
Free Admission
🎨
Saint Louis Art Museum ← click to explore
~10–15 minute drive from downtown
Over 34,000 works spanning 5,000 years of history — from ancient Egypt to modern masters. Perched on Art Hill with one of the best views in the park. A truly stunning building inside and out.
Free Admission
📜
Missouri History Museum ← click to explore
~10–15 minute drive from downtown
Explore the story of St. Louis through immersive exhibits on the 1904 World's Fair, Lewis & Clark, Civil Rights history, and more. A fascinating deep-dive into the Gateway City's past.
Free Admission
🔬
Saint Louis Science Center ← click to explore
~10–15 minute drive from downtown
Hundreds of hands-on exhibits covering space, technology, nature, and the human body. Connected to the James S. McDonnell Planetarium — don't miss the live shows. Great for all ages.
Free Admission
Quick pointers
💡
Quick pointers
  • These excursions are close to downtown — roughly a 10–15 minute drive.
  • The park is open at 6:00 AM and closes at 10:00 PM.
  • Saint Louis Science Center opens at 9:30 AM and closes at 4:30 PM.
  • Missouri History Museum opens at 9:00 AM and closes at 5:00 PM.
  • Saint Louis Art Museum is open from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
  • There is free parking and paid parking for cars ($10).
