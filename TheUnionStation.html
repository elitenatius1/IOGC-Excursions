Union Station Overview
📍 Overview
Union Station is a convenient excursion area with several attractions in one location, making it a great option for Saints who would like to enjoy multiple activities without traveling far between stops.
Helpful distance estimates:
  • From the Renaissance St. Louis Airport Hotel: roughly a 20–25 minute drive to Union Station, depending on traffic.
  • To downtown excursions: Gateway Arch, Old Courthouse, and the Riverfront are roughly 5–10 minutes from Union Station by car.
  • To Forest Park excursions: Forest Park attractions are roughly 10–15 minutes from Union Station if Saints have additional time to explore.
Union Station works especially well as a central stop because Saints can enjoy the Aquarium, Ferris Wheel, Ropes Course, Mirror Maze, and Selfie Express in the same general area, then still head to downtown or Forest Park if time permits.
Anchor Attraction
St. Louis Aquarium ← click to explore
The centerpiece of Union Station, the St. Louis Aquarium features over 13,000 animals across multiple exhibits. Walk through immersive environments including shark habitats, river ecosystems, and interactive touch tanks.

Hours: Typically open daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (hours may vary).

Driving directions
From Union Station, make your way toward I-64 / US-40 or I-70 West in the direction of St. Louis Lambert International Airport. Continue westbound, then follow signs toward Natural Bridge Road near the airport area. Continue to the Renaissance St. Louis Airport Hotel at 9801 Natural Bridge Road, St. Louis, MO 63134.

Estimated drive time: about 20–25 minutes, depending on traffic.
